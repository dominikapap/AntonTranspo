WHO ARE WE?

This is not a real company, but a fake name created for portolio reasons. Althought if it was, the story about how it came to be could be something like that:

It all began in 1980 with one old bus and a dream. The owner, Anton S., built it all from the scratch, working tirereslly and saving for a next vehicle.

The company grew, Anton hired more drivers, bought more buses and moved the center of their operations to Wonderville.

Nowadays Anton Transpo is well known locally as their buses takes children to schools, workers to workplaces and travelers of all kinds to their chosen destinations.

Anton Transpo organizes travels also to other UE countries, mostly to vacation or summer work destinations.
